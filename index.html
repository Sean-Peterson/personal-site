Me

Hello, my name is Sean Peterson. I love a good challenge. I love challenges because they are opportunities for growth and learning. Coding presents new challenges everyday which is why I am passionate about coding.

My coding journey began in 2014. I was a founder of a startup called Cliq. During the startup process I became interested in coding and started messing around on codeacademy.com. The startup failed, but my interest in coding had just begun. I enrolled in Epicodus, a coding bootcamp that is ranked top 5 nationally, located in Portland, Oregon.

At Epicodus we completed over 45 hours of pair-programming per week. We learned git, html5, css, js, angular.js, ember.js, mysql, php, silex, and drupal. After the classes were over I won an internship at SimB & CO. During the internship I had a week to learn how to use Symfony2 framework and Docker CaaS and then develop an entire website with another intern for a paying client. We developed a training website for the National Guard and OHSU. The project required us to architect a database, create controllers, and design views. It was truly a full-stack job.

After completing my classes at Epicodus I lacked confidence and was unsure if I would feel comfortable telling a prospective employer that I will be an asset to the company. After rocking my internship and personal projects, I have full confidence in my ability to code and learn new technologies. Without a doubt I can be a real asset to any company.
